How this started

We’re just a regular family. One day my son bought a bag of chips, opened it, and said, “I got scammed — it’s like one-third chips and the rest is air.”

We laughed, because we’ve all said it before. Every time you buy chips, you’re really buying a bag of air with a few chips at the bottom.

Joking turned into brainstorming. What if someone actually made a chip company that sold full bags of chips? No tricks. No air tax.

Someone said it out loud: “We should call it Bag Full of Chips.” And that was it.

One joke turned into sketches, mockups, and a real plan to build a brand that does the obvious thing: fill the bag.
